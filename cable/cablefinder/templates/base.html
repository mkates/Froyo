About BundleMagic

SmartAutoSavings helps consumers take control of the car buying process. Just tell us the car you are looking for, and we'll deliver competitive price quotes from dealers in your area. It's fast, simple, and free, and there's no obligation.

With multiple dealer quotes to compare, you can be confident that you are getting the best deal possible when you buy.

We are networked to accredited auto dealers nationwide, so that we can conduct a comprehensive search of dealer inventory in any local area and provide low price quotes on the new car you want.

Why waste hours of your time at car dealerships? Let SmartAutoSavings do the legwork for you.

Located north of Boston, our SmartAutoSavings team includes former auto industry executives who have an insider's perspective on how dealers operate. And they believe that the old, traditional car buying process was in need of improvement.
